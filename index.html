Sinotruk International

Sinotruk is manufactured and owned by China National Heavy Duty Truck Group Co., Ltd. It has a full range of commercial vehicle brands including Yellow River, SITRAK, and HOWO. Sinotruk is trusted by construction and mining companies for its strength, reliability, and ability to cope with harsh working conditions. It is exported to over 110 countries.

More than 4 million Sinotruk commercial vehicles serve various markets, supported by over 1,000 sales and service outlets. The international presence includes 67 representative offices, over 400 overseas sales personnel, and 240+ dealers. Additionally, 28 KD factories across 18 countries help meet global demand.

SINOTRUK owns brands like SITRAK, Howo, Hohan, Steyr, and more. It is a leader in China’s heavy truck industry, offering products ranging from light to heavy loads, suitable for both transportation and engineering.
